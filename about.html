About Me

KATIE MARPLE

Hello, my name is Katie Marple and I'm a bilingual British model living in Osaka. I have traveled to over thirty countries, and lived in five. I love playing with different photography styles to achieve the perfect shot.

My method of work is straight forward, I create or discuss a concept, then set a mood board and research the best place to achieve our desired look. I've worked with many photographers across the world. So, if your interested in working with me to create beauitful shots, please get in touch.
