My Portfolio

KATIE MARPLE

I have worked with fellow models and photographers in many countries, from the Costa Rica jungle to Japan. I enjoy exploring different styles and feel the most creative outside. However, I also have lots of studio experience too. Please take a look at a portfolio of some of my work below nd contact me if you'd like to collaborate.

Spring In Japan

This collection was completed in collaboration with photographer Calvin Harris and model Natalie during Spring 2023. It was the first time I was able to photograph and have fun with the plum blossoms, so even though it was very cold (it actually started snowing), nothing would stop us from getting these beautiful shots.

Goddess on the Sands

The stunning outfits showcased here are handmade by a Mexican's womens collective for Marissa's 'Casita Xaman' range. All photos and editing were done by the talented and ethereal photographer and digital artist Marc Gates. We wanted to show the free-flowing movement of the clothes, which are all made from natural organics and embody the essence of goddess.
